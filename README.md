# IEEE IES Hyderabad Hub

## Overview
This repository documents the activities, initiatives, and events organized under the **IEEE Industrial Electronics Society (IES) Hyderabad Hub**, part of the IEEE IES Hubs & Nodes Initiative.  
It serves as a central resource for leadership vision, regional collaborations, technical workshops, and professional development activities.

## About the Hyderabad Hub
The Hyderabad Hub connects academia, industry, Young Professionals, IEEE Student Branches, researchers, and global IEEE IES leadership.  
It emphasizes:
- Industry-academia collaboration  
- Student & faculty empowerment  
- Professional networking & leadership development  
- Chapter & Student Branch growth  
- Hands-on technical activities in emerging domains (EVs, Smart Grids, Industrial AI, Industry 5.0, etc.)

## Key Events

### Chennai – EV Battery & Charging Technology Workshop
- 2-day workshop on EV battery systems & charging infrastructure  
- **Impact:** 289 participants, 7 new student members, Madras Chapter revitalized, new Student Branch Chapter inaugurated  

### Kochi – Faculty Development Programme
- 6-day hybrid FDP on AIoT-enabled pedagogy using MATLAB, Arduino, Raspberry Pi  
- Included industry visits, IEEE IES leadership engagement, and networking  

### Surat – E-Mobility Industry-Academia Conclave
- 1-day conclave at SVNIT Surat  
- Focused on EV ecosystem, industry-academia collaboration, and IEEE IES visibility
